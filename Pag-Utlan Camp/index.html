About Pag-Utlan Camp

Pag-Utlan Camp Resort is a family-friendly getaway that offers a variety of accommodations, including cabins and campsites.

View Accommodations

Accommodations

  • Huts

    Our cozy cabins are perfect for families or couples looking for a romantic retreat.

  • Campsites

    Our spacious campsites are ideal for those who prefer a more rustic experience. Guests have access to nearby restrooms and showers.
View Activities

Activities

  • Biking

    Rent a bike and hit the trails for a thrilling ride through the beautiful countryside.

  • Island Hopping

    Come! Let's hop on different islets each holding instagram worthy bounties and stop on some diving spots to see the wonderful beauty of our marine life with our safe and fun pump boats.

  • Kayaking

    Take to the water and explore the nearby waters in a kayak or canoe. Guests can rent kayaks and canoes from the resort and enjoy a peaceful paddle through the scenic waterways.
